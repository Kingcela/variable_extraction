There could be reflections of this extended structure in low-energy phenomena due to possible mixings of the known particles with the new particles predicted by the model, such as deviations from the V-A form of weak interactions, but nothing of that kind has turned up in experiments so far. There are not very definite mass limits for the right-handed Majorana neutrinos.
