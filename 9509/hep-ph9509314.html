The other idea is to consider this effect as some ”induced” decay of muon in the field of spectator. If this approach is correct, then these muon colliders can be also used as μ⁢ν𝜇𝜈italic-mu italic-nu\mu\nu, ν⁢ν𝜈𝜈italic-nu italic-nu\nu\nu,… colliders with high enough luminosity.
