Abstract

A current of the deformed Virasoro algebra is identified with the Zamolodchikov-Faddeev operator for the basic scalar particle in the XYZ model.

September, 95

The studies of infinite dimensional algebras has turned out to be the main tendency in the recent development of the theory of two-dimensional quantum integrable systems. This approach was originated by the fundamental work of A.A. Belavin, A.M. Polyakov and A.B. Zamolodchikov. Now it has become clear that the Virasoro-type symmetry is not a unique feature of Conformal Field Theory models.
